Israt Zarin Era

Computer Vision | Deep Learning | Data Augmentation | Additive Manufacturing | Healthcare

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Industrial & Management Systems Engineering at West Virginia University, specializing in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Data Augmentation. My research focuses on bridging the gap between advanced machine-learning frameworks and real-life scenarios with unlabeled & limited labeled datasets with class imbalance for complex applications in additive manufacturing, and medical imaging. I develop robust frameworks for data augmentation, semi-supervised, self-supervised learning, and unsupervised segmentation to handle high-dimensional data effectively.

View my LinkedIn Profile View my Google Scholar email: ie0005@mix.wvu.edu

Research Focus

  • Image Segmentation & Classification
  • Anomaly Detection & Defect Detection
  • Data Augmentation & Class Balancing
  • Medical Imaging & Additive Manufacturing AI
